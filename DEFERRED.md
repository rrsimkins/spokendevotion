## Web3Forms Access Key
Contact form is wired to Web3Forms via `partials/contact-form.js`. Replace `YOUR_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY` in `partials/contact-config.js` with your access key from [web3forms.com](https://web3forms.com). In the Web3Forms dashboard, set the notification email to `contact@spokendevotion.com` and enable the autoresponder (Pro plan) with the confirmation message.
