A moment of reflection and encouragement

“In the Quiet Place”

In the quiet place where the world grows still,
Where the noise of life cannot reach my soul,
There I meet You, Lord, in the gentle whisper,
In the hush of Your presence, I am made whole.

You speak peace to the storms that rage within,
You calm the anxious thoughts that steal my rest.
Your Word is a lamp unto my weary feet,
And in Your light, I find my very best.

So I come, not with loud cries or many words,
But with a heart laid bare before Your throne.
Here in the silence, I hear Your voice so clear:
“Child, you are Mine, and you are never alone.”

— Spoken Devotion

A Prayer for Today

Heavenly Father,

Thank You for the gift of Your Word and the power of spoken prayer. Today I lay down my burdens, my fears, and my distractions at Your feet. Speak to my heart in the quiet moments. Fill me with Your peace that surpasses understanding. Help me to trust You more, to love others as You have loved me, and to walk in the light of Your truth.

In Jesus’ precious name, Amen.

— A simple prayer from our hearts to yours
