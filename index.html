Who We Are

Spoken Devotion is not a church, charity, or official ministry. We are a family of ordinary working people who love God — creating worship, prayer, poetry, and Scripture in the hours around our daily lives, and sharing it freely with anyone who might be encouraged.

We are not here to ask anything of you. We do not solicit donations, and we are not seeking support, recognition, or reward. We simply want to put God’s love into the world through what He has given us — the same way a neighbor might share a meal or a word of hope.

If something here touches your heart and you feel moved to give, we would lovingly invite you to turn that generosity toward someone near you: a neighbor in need, a friend carrying a heavy burden, a stranger who could use a practical act of kindness. Let your gift become God’s love made visible right where you live — for that is devotion lived out, not only spoken.

“Let us not love with words or speech alone, but with actions and in truth.” — 1 John 3:18
