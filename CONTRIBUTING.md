# Contributing to Spoken Devotion

Thank you for your interest in Spoken Devotion! This is a personal ministry project focused on creating spoken-word content that points people to Jesus through worship, prayer, confessions, and Scripture.

## How You Can Help

While this is primarily a personal project, contributions and feedback are welcome in these areas:

- Suggesting new content (prayers, worship songs, confessions, or Bible narrations)
- Reporting bugs or issues with the website
- Suggesting improvements to design, user experience, or mobile experience
- Technical improvements (performance, accessibility, etc.)
