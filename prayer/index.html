A Spoken Prayer for Strength

Lord Jesus, in moments of weakness, be my strength.
When anxiety rises, speak Your peace over my heart.
When doubt clouds my mind, remind me of Your faithfulness.
Fill me with Your Holy Spirit that I may walk in power and love.

Thank You for never leaving me. Amen.

— Spoken from the heart

Prayer of Repentance

Written June 19, 2026 • Recited by Anna

Father in heaven,

I come before You with empty hands and a shattered heart.

I have sinned against You.
I have turned my back on Your goodness and chosen my own dark way.
My sin is ever before me — it tears at my soul and leaves me in agony.
I groan and weep under the unbearable weight I created for myself.

Have mercy on me, O God, according to Your unfailing love.
Blot out my transgressions.
Wash me thoroughly from my iniquity
and cleanse me from my sin.

I am sorry, Father — I am so deeply sorry.
I am sorry for the doubt that poisoned my mind and made me question Your love.
I am sorry for the sin I kept choosing even when it destroyed me.
I am sorry for the anger that raged inside me and wounded everyone around me.
I am sorry for the running — running from Your presence, running from Your truth, running until I was completely lost and empty.
I am sorry for the pain I caused — the pain in Your heart, the pain I brought into the lives of those I love, and the crushing pain I brought upon my own soul.

Jesus, my Lord and Savior,
remember me in this brokenness.
Forgive me.
Wash me clean in Your blood.
Make me whole again.

Create in me a clean heart, O God,
and renew a right spirit within me.
Do not cast me away from Your presence.
Restore to me the joy of Your salvation
and uphold me with Your Spirit.

I am Yours, Father.
I turn back to You now with tears and trembling.
I have nowhere else to go.
Receive me, hold me, heal me, and make me new.

For Yours is the kingdom,
Yours is the power,
and Yours is the glory,
forever and ever.

In Jesus’ name I pray,
Amen.

— Spoken word prayer inspired by God’s Word
