Reflections

Contemporary songs that reflect God, faith, and life with Him.

Reflections is our space for contemporary-style music and devotion — original songs written in a modern voice that reflect something true about God, about struggle, about hope, and about walking through life with Him.

These are not worship songs in the sense of singing directly to the Lord, like our Worship section. They are personal reflections — honest, melodic, and God-centered — meant to help you pause, feel, and remember that He is near in the ordinary moments too.
