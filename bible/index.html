About the Spoken Devotion Version (SDV)

The Spoken Devotion Version of the Bible is translated from the original Hebrew, Aramaic, and Greek texts using Grok Translate, then carefully edited for clarity and easy readability. It is an original devotional translation — not copied, adapted, or derived from any other published Bible translation.
